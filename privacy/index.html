  1. Introduction

Welcome to Mayclear. We, our, or us refers to Mayclear. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, the App. Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

  1. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials.

Task Data
Tasks, notes, and productivity data you create or import from connected services.

Voice Data
Audio recordings when you use voice input features. These recordings are processed to convert speech to text and are not stored permanently on our servers.

Integration Data
When you connect third party services such as Trello, Notion, Todoist, Google Tasks, or other task management tools, we access and sync your task data from those services.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data
How you interact with the App, including features used, session duration, and in app actions.

Device Information
Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

Log Data
Error logs and performance data to improve App stability.

2.3 AI Processing

Voice Conversations
When you use our AI voice coaching feature, your voice input is processed by AI services such as Google Gemini to provide real time responses. Voice data is processed in real time and is not stored after the session ends.

Task Analysis
We use AI to analyze your tasks and provide personalized recommendations, breakdowns, and time estimates.

  1. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve the App, process and sync your tasks across connected services, provide AI powered coaching, task breakdowns, and productivity insights, send you notifications and reminders you have configured, respond to your inquiries and provide customer support, monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience, and detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats.

  1. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances.

4.1 Service Providers

We use third party services to help operate our App, including Supabase for database and authentication services, Google Cloud and Gemini API for AI processing for voice coaching, and Expo and EAS for app build and distribution services.

4.2 Third Party Integrations

When you connect external services such as Trello, Notion, or Todoist, your task data is shared between Mayclear and those services according to their respective privacy policies.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

  1. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of data in transit using TLS or SSL, secure authentication protocols, regular security assessments, and limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only.

  1. Data Retention

Account Data
Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for backup and legal purposes.

Voice Data
Processed in real time and not stored after transcription.

Task Data
Retained until you delete it or close your account.

Usage Analytics
Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely.

  1. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights.

Access
Request a copy of your personal data.

Correction
Request correction of inaccurate data.

Deletion
Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data. You may request complete deletion of your account at any time by contacting us. Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete your account and all data within 30 days.

Export
Request your data in a portable format.

Opt Out
Disable certain data collection through App settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy at mayclear dot app.

  1. Childrens Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

  1. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.

  1. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the Last Updated date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

  1. California Privacy Rights CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

  1. European Privacy Rights GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. Our legal basis for processing is your consent and legitimate interests in providing the service.
